Choosing the right appliance repair service is important for saving time and money.

1. **Check reviews** — see what people say on Google and Yelp.  
2. **Ask about warranty** — good companies provide at least 90 days.  
3. **Verify licensing** — make sure they are certified in your state.

> When searching for *dryer repair near me*, pick local experts who service your ZIP area.
